Return & Replace

If you want to return or replace a product, fill the form below and press Send Request on WhatsApp. Our team will respond and you can attach photos on WhatsApp for faster processing.

Please attach clear photos of the product & packaging on WhatsApp after opening the chat — attachments cannot be sent via this form.

Return & Replace Policy (brief)
  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery for manufacturing defects only.
  • Replacement / refund processed after inspection.
  • Customer must share clear photos on WhatsApp for faster approval.
  • Products must be unused (except to verify defect) and with original packaging.

After sending, open WhatsApp and attach photos — our team will guide next steps.
